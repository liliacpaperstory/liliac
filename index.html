Our Story

Born from a love of the written word

Liliac began in a small Vancouver apartment with a single idea: stationery should feel as meaningful as the words written on it. We believe in slowing down, putting pen to paper, and choosing gifts that speak from the heart.

Every product we create is designed in-house, printed on quality paper, and packaged with care — because the details matter.

🍁 Proudly Canadian-owned & operated since 2019
